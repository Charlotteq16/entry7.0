My Thoughts

"Alot of people suck, don't be like them" When I saw this I laughed a little and thought it was funny, but at the same time its so true. For alot of my life I was what is considered to be a push over. I am someone who hates confrontation and never like to cause problems, so because of that alot of people would treat me unfairly because they know I would never say anything about it, because I never want to cause any problems. It wasn't until very recently this year I realized I deserve the same amount of respect I consistently give to other people. I was taught that keeping quiet kept the peace, until I realized whose peace it was keeping, not mine, everyone elses.
